"Food is not sustenance alone — it is memory, ecology, resistance, and time."

This archive maps the living, breathing culinary knowledge of Adivasi and Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) across West Bengal's most ecologically distinct territories. Each entry layers spatial geography with community identity, wild ingredient knowledge, ancestral cooking technologies, seasonal rhythms, oral folklore, and scholarly sources — creating a multi-dimensional portrait of food as cultural survival.
